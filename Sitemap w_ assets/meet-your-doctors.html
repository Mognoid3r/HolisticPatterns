At Holistic Patterns, we’re a team of affiliate doctors, nutritionists, health coaches, fitness specialist and generally people who care about helping you along your health journey. With a blend of conventional, evidence-based approaches and innovative thinking, we help patients address a wide-variety of health questions from preventative medicine to diabetes to autoimmune disease to metabolic syndrome and much more.

 

We want every patient through every step of the process to feel more informed, and more empowered take control of their health than before we met.

 

Here are the partners ready to help you every step of the way:

Affiliated Functional Doctors Health Coach Nutrition & Fitness Affiliate Doctors, Coaches and More
  • With a focus on treating the whole patient rather than just the symptoms, our network of affiliate doctors deliver innovative, individualized, and collaborative evidence-based care in health and telemedicine. Conventional testing methods and evidence-based medicines help each patient safely achieve their health goals holistically.
  • Search our network of doctors to find the partner that you need. Anytime, anywhere.
  • Our health coaches will help you get to the root of your ailment and reshape your patterns into a more balanced and holistic life. We are your support system at your fingertips and will help with everything from counseling to nutrition to motivation to fitness. Our goal: Help you reach your health & wellness goals.
  • We treat food as medicine, as we develop individualized nutritional plans based on your unique labs, ailments and goals. After the program is developed, we don’t forget about you. We keep you motivated by setting goals, creating health strategies, providing guidance needed to keep you active and hold you accountable to your plan.
  • In addition to our holistic programs, health coaches and other shop offers, we also partner with a strong network of licensed, vetted and experienced network of affiliate doctors, fitness coaches and more who are passionate and focused on providing you with holistic health.
